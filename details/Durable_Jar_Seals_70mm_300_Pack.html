Protect Your Jars with Leak-Proof, Durable Lid Caps

Keep your kitchen organized and your containers sealed with confidence using our 300-Pack of premium lid caps. Designed with durable, weatherproof material, these lids ensure your food, spices, and other items stay fresh and free from unwanted odors.

Triple-Layer Protection for Maximum Safety

Each PS foam seal offers a reliable defense against leaks, tampering, and evaporation. The sealed paperwork backing keeps the foam lid clean and prevents dust from settling or causing mutual adhesion, guaranteeing long-lasting performance for every use.

Versatile and Easy to Use

These lid caps are perfect for all types of jars, bottles, and tumblers, making them ideal for both home and professional kitchens. Simply clean the jar's surface, place the foam lid with the white side down, apply steady pressure, and screw it securely into place.
